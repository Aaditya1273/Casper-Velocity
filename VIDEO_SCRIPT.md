## 🎯 PSYCHOLOGICAL FRAMEWORK

**Hook**: Start with a problem so big, judges feel the pain  
**Tension**: Build urgency with real numbers

## 🎬 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visual Style
- **Dark theme** with Arbitrum blue accents
- **Smooth animations** - nothing jarring
- **Real footage** of the app working (not mockups)
- **Live blockchain data** - show actual transactions
- **Professional graphics** - think Apple keynote quality

### Pacing
- **0:00-0:45**: Fast-paced, building tension
- **0:45-1:15**: Slower, confident, showing solution
- **1:15-1:45**: Authoritative, proving credibility
- **1:45-2:15**: Inspirational, painting vision
- **2:15-2:30**: Direct, call to action

### Music
- **0:00-0:45**: Tense, building (think Inception)
- **0:45-1:15**: Triumphant, solution-oriented
- **1:15-2:15**: Epic, confident (think Marvel)
- **2:15-2:30**: Powerful, decisive

### Tone of Voice
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Urgent** but not desperate
- **Technical** but accessible
- **Visionary** but grounded in reality
